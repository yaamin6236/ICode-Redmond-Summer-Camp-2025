Baseball

Baseball is a classic and strategic sport played between two teams of nine players. Teams alternate between offense and defense, aiming to score runs by hitting a pitched ball and running around four bases: first, second, third, and home. The pitcher throws the ball to the batter, who tries to hit it and reach base safely. The team with the most runs at the end of the game wins. Baseball is known for its rich history, teamwork, and exciting moments that bring fans together.
