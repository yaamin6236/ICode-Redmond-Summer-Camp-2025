Tennis

Tennis is a dynamic and exciting sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net. Players use a racket to hit a ball over the net, aiming to land it within the opponent's court. Points are scored when the opponent cannot return the ball or hits it out of bounds. Tennis requires agility, strategy, and precision, making it both challenging and thrilling for players and spectators alike.
