Soccer

Soccer is the world's most popular sport, played between two teams of eleven players with a round ball. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing team's net using any part of the body except the hands and arms (except for the goalkeeper). Soccer is known for its fast pace, teamwork, and global appeal, captivating fans and players of all ages.
