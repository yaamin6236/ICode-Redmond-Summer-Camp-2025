What is Crochet?

Crochet is a craft which is made by taking yarn and creating it into a 3D structure. It turns loops into knots, and knots into fabric. It can be traced from many places such as Spain, Arabia, parts of the Mediterranean, China, and South America. Crocheting requires one hook but knitting needs two needles. Crochet is a version of knitting. It can be fun but difficult. Counting stiches is the hardest part. You have to look for a v shape in the stiches going in a certain direction. When you finish a project is can be very rewarding. Crocheting can be a hobby or a side job! Many people sell their work online on shops like Etsy or Instagram. Crochet can be used to make many things. When making small toys or stuffed animals it is called amigurumi. It is a versatile craft that allows for creativity and personalization in every project. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced crocheter, there is always something new to learn and create in the world of crochet.
