Materials

The materials you will need are hooks macthing with the yarn size. So if there was a size 4.5mm the yarn should also be 4.5mm in thickness. You will see this on the side of the piece of paper going over the yarn. You will also need stuffing and saftey eyes for some projects. Yarn needles and stich markers are necessary. It is also good to have scissors to cut the yarn. Somtimes you will need project based materials like buttons or a tape measure. For making earrings you will need earring hooks. If you are making clothing you might need zippers. Tension rings are also good to have, they help you keep a consistent tension in your yarn.
