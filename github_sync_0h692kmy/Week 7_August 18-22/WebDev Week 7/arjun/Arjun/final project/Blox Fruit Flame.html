Blox Fruits: Flame Guide

Overview

Flame Fruit (Mera Mera no Mi) Overview Type: Logia (Elemental) Rarity: Common to Rare Price: Around 2,000,000 Beli or 1,000 Robux (prices vary) Obtained: Can be purchased from the Blox Fruit Dealer Sometimes found as rare drops on certain islands or bosses Tradeable between players Powers and Abilities The Flame Fruit grants the ability to transform into fire, allowing the user to produce, control, and become flames. It offers strong offensive capabilities and mobility. Main Abilities: Fire Transformation: You become fire, allowing immunity to normal physical attacks (except Haki or devil fruit counters). Fire Attacks: Shoot fireballs Create flame explosions Dash with fire to close gaps or escape Mobility: Dash moves leave fiery trails; high mobility with aerial combos. Burn Effects: Attacks inflict burn damage over time on enemies. Moveset Examples [Z] Fireball: Fires a fast projectile that explodes on impact. [X] Fire Spin: Creates a spinning fire attack hitting enemies around you. [C] Fire Dash: Dash forward surrounded by flames, damaging enemies on contact. [F] Flame Burst: Area explosion of fire causing heavy AoE damage. Advantages Good ranged and melee options with burn damage. Logia intangibility provides strong defense. Great for PvE grinding and PvP fights. High mobility with dash and flight capabilities. Weaknesses Vulnerable to Haki users and other Devil Fruit powers that counter Logia.
