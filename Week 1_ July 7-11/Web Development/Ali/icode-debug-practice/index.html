Arenal Costa Rica

Positioned within Costa Rica's fertile northern lowlands, the Arenal Volcano is the most active volcano in Costa Rica. It is a stratovolcano that is 1,633 meters tall and is the most visited tourist attraction in Costa Rica. The volcano is located in the Arenal National Park and is a popular destination for tourists.
