Seattle Seahawks Legion of Boom

The Legion of Boom was the nickname given to the Seattle Seahawks' dominant secondary during the 2010s, particularly from 2012-2015. It was widely considered one of the best defenses in NFL history.

Seattle Seahawks 43-8 Super Bowl win over Broncos

The Seattle Seahawks won their first Super Bowl, Super Bowl XLVIII, by defeating the Denver Broncos 43-8. The game was played on February 2, 2014, and the Seahawks dominated from start to finish. The Seahawks' defense was particularly impressive, holding the Broncos' high-powered offence to just 8 points.
