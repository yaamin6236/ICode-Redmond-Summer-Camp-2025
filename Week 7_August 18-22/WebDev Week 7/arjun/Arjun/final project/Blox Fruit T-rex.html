Blox Fruits: T-rex Guide

Overview

T-Rex Fruit (T-Rex no Mi) Overview Type: Mythical Zoan-type Rarity: Mythical (one of the rarest fruits) Price: Around 8,000,000 Beli or 4,000 Robux Obtained: Can be purchased from the Blox Fruit Dealer (rarely available) Drops randomly in raids or events Can be traded between players, but very expensive Powers and Abilities The T-Rex Fruit allows the user to transform into a giant Tyrannosaurus Rex, gaining massive strength, size, and destructive power. Main Abilities: Transformation: Become a massive T-Rex, greatly increasing size and physical strength. Melee Power: Powerful punches, tail swipes, and bites with high damage and knockback. Roar Ability: Can stun or intimidate enemies around. Durability: High HP and defense while transformed. Charge Attack: Rush forward to deal damage and knock enemies back. Advantages Exceptional melee damage and crowd control. Great for PvE grinding and tanking mobs. Large hitbox allows hitting multiple enemies at once. Can break through certain defenses and abilities. Weaknesses Slow movement speed due to large size. Large hitbox makes dodging harder. Limited ranged attacks. Transformation drains energy over time.
