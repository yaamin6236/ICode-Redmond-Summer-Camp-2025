Blox Fruits: Buddha Guide

Overview

Buddha Fruit (Soru Soru no Mi) Overview Type: Mythical Zoan-type Rarity: Very Rare / Mythical Price: Around 9,000,000 Beli or 4,000 Robux Obtained: Can be bought from the Blox Fruit Dealer Occasionally drops during certain raids or events Can be traded between players Powers and Abilities The Buddha Fruit allows the user to transform into a giant golden Buddha statue, increasing their size, defense, and strength significantly. This makes it a tanky fruit with great crowd control and durability. Main Abilities: Transformation: Turn into a massive Buddha statue, which boosts HP and defense. Area Attacks: Powerful AoE attacks that knock back and stun enemies. Enhanced Melee: Giant fist and punch attacks deal heavy damage with a wide area. Special Moves: Palm Strike: A charged area attack that sends out shockwaves. Buddha Smite: A ground slam that causes massive AoE damage. Meditation: Passive HP regeneration during transformation. Advantages High durability and HP, making it excellent for tanking and grinding mobs. Great crowd control with AoE stuns and knockbacks. Useful in both PvP and PvE due to its tankiness and damage. Can intimidate opponents due to size and reach. Weaknesses Slow movement speed compared to other fruits. Large hitbox makes it easier to target. Transformation consumes energy over time, so managing it is key. Somewhat limited ranged attacks.
