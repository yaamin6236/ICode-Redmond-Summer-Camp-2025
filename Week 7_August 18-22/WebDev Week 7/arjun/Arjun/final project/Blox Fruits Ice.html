Blox Fruits: Ice Fruit Guide

Overview

Ice Fruit (Hie Hie no Mi) Type: Elemental Fruit Rarity: Very Rare / Legendary Price: Usually expensive, depending on the server and trading market Obtained: Found randomly from Blox Fruit spawns every hour or bought/traded with other players Powers and Abilities: Eating the Ice Fruit gives the user ice-based powers, allowing them to freeze enemies and control ice in battle. It’s great for crowd control and dealing consistent damage. Main Abilities: Ice Wave – Sends out a wave of ice that damages and can freeze opponents. Ice Pillar – Creates pillars of ice to attack or block. Ice Slide – Allows fast movement by sliding on ice. Ice Prison – Traps enemies in blocks of ice, immobilizing them temporarily. Advantages: Can freeze opponents, making it easier to land combos. Good for both PvP and PvE. Strong crowd control with freezing effects. Decent range attacks. Weaknesses: Ice attacks are weaker against Fire-type fruits (like Flame Fruit). Some abilities have cooldowns that need timing. Requires skill to master timing and positioning.

Abilities

  • Ice Spears: Launches sharp ice spears at enemies.
  • Ice Surge: Creates a wave of ice that damages and freezes targets.
