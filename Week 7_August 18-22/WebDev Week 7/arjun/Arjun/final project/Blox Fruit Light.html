Blox Fruits: Light Fruit Guide

Overview

Light Fruit (Pika Pika no Mi) Type: Elemental Fruit Rarity: Very Rare / Legendary Price: Quite expensive, highly sought after by players Obtained: Drops from the Blox Fruit spawn every hour or via player trading Powers and Abilities: The Light Fruit grants the user the power of light, allowing incredibly fast movement and powerful, light-based attacks. It’s known for being one of the fastest and most mobile fruits in the game. Main Abilities: Light Speed Dash – Moves at lightning-fast speeds across the map, great for chasing or escaping. Light Beam – Fires a long-range beam of concentrated light energy to damage enemies. Light Clone – Creates a clone that attacks enemies or distracts them. Laser Rain – Unleashes multiple laser beams from the sky to hit multiple targets. Advantages: Exceptional speed and mobility, excellent for both PvP and PvE. Long-range attacks with high damage output. Versatile — good for aggressive or defensive playstyles. Abilities have great range and can hit multiple enemies. Weaknesses: Usually has lower durability, so you need to avoid getting hit. Can be tricky to master because of the speed mechanics. Some abilities require precise timing and positioning.
