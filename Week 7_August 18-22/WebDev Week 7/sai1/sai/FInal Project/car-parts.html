Car Parts

Car parts make up the intricate and interconnected systems that allow a vehicle to function safely, efficiently, and comfortably. Major systems include the engine, transmission, suspension, braking, steering, electrical, and interior/exterior components.

  • Engine: Pistons, crankshaft, camshaft, valves
  • Transmission: Manual/automatic gears, clutch, differential
  • Suspension: Struts, shocks, springs, control arms
  • Braking: Pads, rotors, calipers, ABS, ESC
  • Steering: Hydraulic/electric systems, rack and pinion
  • Electrical: Battery, alternator, ECU, sensors
  • Cooling: Radiator, thermostat, water pump
  • Fuel System: Tank, pump, injectors, filters
  • Interior/Exterior: Seats, dashboard, HVAC, body panels, mirrors

Modern cars rely on electronic control units (ECUs) for engine performance, climate control, and safety features. Every part must work harmoniously for safety, efficiency, and comfort.
